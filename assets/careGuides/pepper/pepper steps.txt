1.Plant pepper seeds or seedlings in a sunny location with well-draining soil.
2.Water consistently to keep the soil evenly moist, but avoid waterlogged conditions.
3.Fertilize with a balanced fertilizer to support healthy growth and fruit development.
4.Provide support for the plants as they grow taller, using stakes or cages.
5.Harvest ripe peppers regularly to encourage continuous fruiting and plant productivity.
